MSR College of Science and Technology

MSR University was established on 30th April, 1999 as a unitary type of University.This University was named as MSR the abbreviation is M.S.RISSHIVARAN,the man who got Space Pioneer Awards. It ,offers higher education in Engineering, Technology, Architecture and Applied Sciences relevant to the current and projected needs of the society. Besides promoting research and disseminating knowledge gained therefrom, it fosters cooperation between the academic and industrial communities.
